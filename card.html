Snow Leopard

The global snow leopard population is estimated to be just 3,500 to 6,500 today and continues to decrease.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera Uncia
  • Average Weight: 77 to 120lbs
  • Average Lifespan: 15-18 years
  • Habitat: Mountians of Central Asia

Snow Leopards are very independent and solitary creatures, living alone most of the time and only socializing during the mating season.
